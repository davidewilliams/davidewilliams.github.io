Status Update – March 17, 2020
Lesa went into work, I worked from home and watched the kids. I got 7.5 hours of work done which is kind of amazing considering. In the morning the boys went outside and played in the mud until I chased them out of the yard and into the woods to dig. Ashley played in her room with … well, I’m not sure what. We had Vienna Sausages for lunch, their pick, I ate the barbecue ones, had pop-cycles afterward. read more
Status Update – March 16, 2020
Bizarre day to say the least. Lesa went into work. I got kids ready and took them to daycare and then I went into work. Not many people at work, one co-worker came in, said he didn’t feel well, and left. Two others left midday leaving me by myself. I worked a full day and then picked up the kids. Annetta said they were all good and the next time they’re at daycare she’ll get them some ice cream. read more
Status Update – March 15, 2020
I woke up in a fever, took my temp, it was okay. Church canceled Sunday School, evening worship, Wednesday activities, everything but 10:30 worship service. They live-streamed the service. They also changed the way we do communion and offering. Pretty sparse attendance. We kept the kids with us. Right after church we went to Walmart and stocked up on more stuff. Couldn’t find cereal or bread so hit three Dollar Generals on the way home from Walmart and still didn’t find any. read more
Status Update – March 14, 2020
Stayed home all morning. I was listening to Flatt & Scruggs Live at Neely Auditorium on Vanderbilt University in Nashville, TN from May 2, 1963 and that kind of got the kids fired up. It turned into YouTube and hip-hop and others, and that of course turned into dancing and videos. The pinewood derby was supposed to be today but was canceled so we did something different. Played Words with Friends game (it’s like Scrabble) with Ashley and only beat her by 6 points. read more
Status Update – March 13, 2020
School and work. Had a hard time getting motivated at work. Was going to leave early but wound up staying later. School robocalled to say school is closed due to COVID-19 and won’t be open until April. When I picked up kids I talked to Jessica about what we could expect daycare to do and it looks like unless sickness hits them it will be an option for us. Both our workplaces are being flexible so I think we’ll be okay. read more
Status Update – March 12, 2020
School and work. Lots of COVID-19 talk everywhere, it’s on everyone’s mind, most people trying not to panic, some people are simply idiots. Wondering whether we should cancel our Spring Break trip, Lesa looking to me to keep us all safe, kids are blissfully unaware of the danger. Getting pressure to step up my game at work; the office is more than half empty. Ditto for Lesa’s work, many people wanting to or already are working from home. read more
Status Update – March 11, 2020
Woke up exhausted, didn’t want to get up, finally did. Stayed exhausted throughout the day, wound up working over even so. Jason texted and wanted to come to our house to cut pinewood derby cars, got home and he was already waiting in our drive, we cut the cars and the boys painted already, I polished the axles so they might go faster. Lesa went and got milk after dinner. Kids on devices or taking showers. read more
Status Update – March 10, 2020
Took kids to school. Spent the entire day at SAIC. Lesa back at work, sent me email IU is closing classes after Spring Break and almost everyone will be working remotely. Pulled a nine hour day and then picked up kids from daycare, I’m utterly exhausted. Slept much of the evening on the couch. Kids watched television and played and made much noise which irritated me. Jason Sowder was going to cut pinewood derby cars for me and the boys but cancelled earlier in the afternoon, hopefully tomorrow. read more
Status Update – March 9, 2020
Took kids to school. Worked half a day at Scientia, other half at SAIC. Lesa took the day off, DST blues. She went shopping for supplies, hopefully enough to get us through two weeks of Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) epidemic but I doubt it. No troubles at school or daycare. Picked kids up and came home, Lesa had supper made. We ate and I took the boys to Cub Scouts. Dwyane packed care packages for the Bedford Police Department. read more
Status Update – March 8, 2020
Everyone more tired than usual due to DST and losing an hour. Went to church, didn’t serve or teach. After lunch Lesa went to Rural King in Bedford looking for a larger drop pan which they didn’t have, and she went to CVS as well. Ashley had a meltdown over her birthday and the amount of gifts she can have. The kids rode bikes a lot, it was a beautiful day. read more
