Status Update – September 14, 2019
Woke up about 4:00am in tent, took boys to bathroom, went back to sleep until 6:20am. Got up, another parent started stoking fire, I broke out camp stove and started coffee. Wound up making four pots for folks, and scrambling six dozen eggs. Dwyane ate nearly an entire bag of donuts, Bruce ate what he didn’t. Broke camp after breakfast and took boys to Shoot-O-Ree. Their favorite things were fishing rod casting, Marshmallow shooters, and catapults. read more
