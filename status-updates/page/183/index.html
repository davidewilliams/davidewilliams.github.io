Status Update – September 25, 2016
Scheduled for Jr. Church and Security Deacon, latter won out. Got angry with Lesa and told her/kids to get out of my truck, they drove in Jeep. Said sorry to kids at dinner, explained I was wrong. Spent afternoon cleaning recyclables. Lesa took Ashley to get photo album. Bad day.
Status Update – September 26, 2016
Dillan, myself have flu; Dillan puked on bedroom carpet; Dwyane upset stomach; Lesa, Ashley, Bruce okay; took off work. Pulled Ashley from school for pock marks on face/body, went to doctor, she has impetigo, released for school. Stephanie picked up paperwork. Got a little sleep.
Status Update – September 27, 2016
Everyone feels better, even me, though still sick. Spent lot of time on financial Adoption Questionnaire. Got Dillan’s IEP for Stephanie. Scanned dentist, doctor statements. Dillan had ton of homework! Got questionnaire back from Tammy, spent evening with Lesa revising. So tired!
Status Update – September 28, 2016
Full work day. Kids normal. Felt okay, not 100%. Left on time, picked up kids, Lesa already home. Sort of helped Ashley with homework. Tried to nap before dinner, able to after. Lesa helped Dillan, did good with homework from yesterday. Watched TV. Summer over, turned furnace on.
Status Update – September 29, 2016
Thursday dragged on and on, left work soon as possible. First day this week without adoption stuff to do, we must be getting close to being done! Scheduled parent teacher conferences. Mrs. Wronowicz sent photos of Ashley. Kids rambunctious, rainy and cold so they couldn’t go out.
Status Update – September 3, 2016
Day started bad, got worse. Kids drove me nuts. They’re demons! Dillan literally strangling siblings. Bruce kicked me. Dwyane mad we made him go out, said he’s running away, sat at end of drive for hour, then threw fit when time to come in! I yelled a lot, ready to call it quits!
Status Update – September 30, 2016
Friday! Worked 7 hours, took PTO for Monday. Got home, gutters Mike Prince ordered laying in front yard. Started cleaning for Amber’s visit. Lesa called, Jeep won’t start, went to Bloomington with kids, picked her up, left jeep, will check out in AM. Pizza! Amber visited 2 hours!
Status Update – September 6, 2017
Lesa stayed home again, worried about her state of mind, stressed or worn out? Dillan said sh*t in daycare van, denied it. Bruce said motherf*cker, hit two teachers, got hit by another kid, and generally misbehaved, most likely has poison ivy around right eye. All to bed on time!
