Status Update – July 7, 2018
Up at 7am for breakfast. Art and crafts: made tie-dye shirts, painted Tic-tac-toe board, Ashley made yarn doll. Canoeing, even Lesa! Lunch. Lesa napped, kids and me went swimming. Archery. Trip into Poland for Slushies. Dinner. Swam more. Campfire. Raccoons. What a day! Worn out!
Status Update – July 6, 2018
Lesa PTO again, I worked from home again. Lesa spent day prepping for camp. I packed truck, got a bunch of tickets, finished them, and off we went to Camp Na Wa Kwa for weekend: checked in, unpacked truck, settled into Yurt, ate dinner, went swimming, built campfire, ate S’mores!
Status Update – July 5, 2018
Lesa PTO, said kids could stay with her, no daycare, thought she’d need help, worked from home. Kids played inside/outside, went swimming, Dillan rode dirt bike, etc. Lesa went grocery shopping. Tried to get kids to nap, should’ve known better. Traded rental car for rental truck.
Status Update – July 4, 2018
Bruce nightmares about curtains. Fairfax Beach, picnic lunch, swam all afternoon, Lesa and I burnt, kids fine. Got Suburban back! Bob Evans cooked so Lesa didn’t have to, everyone hungry, we chowed! Watched Coco movie until dusk. Set off lots of fireworks, it was totally awesome!
Status Update – July 3, 2018
Daycare, left work early to get Ashley at camp, Lesa picked up boys. Talked to Marcy Cook and Steve Johnson at Hilltop. Took Ashley to Casa Brava in Bedford for dinner. Talked about camp: she likes camp, had fun, wants to go back next year for full week. Enjoyed talking with her!
Kid Quote of the Day – July 3, 2018
Dwyane: Mommy, can we sing Happy Birthday to Dillan again? Lesa: Why? Dwyane: Because it’s Dillan’s birthday and I like his birthday. Lesa: You can sing Happy Birthday to him anytime you want. [Dwyane sings Happy Birthday to Dillan again.] Dillan: All this Happy Birthday singing is getting annoying. They sang to me at church too. Lesa: They knew it was your birthday? Status Update – July 2, 2018
Just me and boys. Thunderstorm in AM, power went off at work. Boys went swimming at Mitchell pool. Lesa renewed rental. Sent Scientia paperwork. Photos/videos of Ashley at camp, rope swing, rock wall, Eleanor. Indian-style supper, yummy! Lesa played new Monopoly game with Dillan.
Status Update – July 1, 2018
Church with kids, Lesa stayed home again. Dillan’s birthday party, 9 years old, happy with gifts! Nicolle wrote Happy 10th on Facebook, doesn’t know his age. Swimming, even Lesa. Drove Ashley to Hilltop Camp, signed her in, said goodbyes, nervous but Marcy and Eleanor were there.
Status Update – June 30, 2018
Dillan and Ashley lost their first tournament game, team did unusually bad, season over. Steak ‘n’ Shake lunch. I took kids home while Lesa did last-minute birthday gift shopping for Dillan. Limestone Heritage Parade in Bedford, Dillan on Cub Scout float. Hot! Glad kids survived!
Status Update – June 29, 2018
Offered Scientia job, need to negotiate salary etc. before giving notice, Lesa’s happy. Picked up Bruce again and took to pool. Lesa went shopping for Ashley’s camp stuff. I got pizzas and kids. Bruce and Dwyane had last game, got trophies. Dillan and Ashley had makeup game, won!
