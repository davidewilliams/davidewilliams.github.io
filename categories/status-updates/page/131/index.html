Status Update – May 9, 2016
Worked long day, tired from busy weekend. Reserved Springville Park shelter house for Ashley’s BD party. Dillan’s ballgame cancelled 1⁄2 hour before start. Scouts instead, Ashley too. Earned Tiger Tales belt loop. Got filthy on playground after, needed showers! Homework, bedtime.
Status Update – May 8, 2016
Mother’s Day, kids made cards for Lesa, they’re sweet! They truly love her! Ray Hausler preached sermon tailor-made for us, almost balled my eyes out. Dwyane actually had an appetite, seems to be doing better. Lesa went to Walmart, got Dwyane, Bruce new cars. Kids played all day.
Status Update – May 7, 2016
Campfire coffee, breakfast, fishing! Broke camp, headed home, Bonehead’s lunch! Shower! Kids and Lesa glad I’m home, me too! Dillan’s first ballgame! He did awesome! Jiffy Treat after game. Naps! Kids rode bikes and played outside except Dwyane: kept him in and let him rest. Bed!
Status Update – May 6, 2016
Teacher called, Dwyane’s sick, we knew, Lesa took him to doctor, told her allergies, bull crap, he’s sick! Loaded truck with gear and Dillan, bought bait, headed to Camp Maumee. Set up camp, ate beef stew, best stew ever! Played, hiked, S’mores, campfire, sleeping bags/tent, fun!
Status Update – May 5, 2016
On time; Dwyane doing better; kids had Mother’s Day stuff for me to hide. Took Dillan to baseball practice, team River Dogs, he got his navy blue shirt and hat, he strutted and showed his stuff, did pretty well playing. Came home, packed all the camping stuff except for the food.
Status Update – May 4, 2016
Ran late this AM. Ashley took field trip to Sieboldt Quarry. Dwyane peed his clothes at daycare yesterday and today, wet bed last night; he’s sick with a low-grade fever. Worked extra 2 hours. Took Dillan to Walmart, spent $400 on camping gear for Scout camping trip this weekend.
Status Update – May 3, 2016
Primary elections held in North Lawrence Schools – no school for all kids – even Dwyane. They loved it. Allowed for more leisurely than normal morning routine, good because kids up late last night due to board meeting. Tomorrow will be hard because they’ll want to play some more.
Status Update – May 2, 2016
Back to work / school / daycare, no one wanted to go. Tired, didn’t sleep last night. Dillan’s ballgame canceled because of rain; pictures, first game, jamboree on Saturday – we hope! Went to C3 board meeting, took all the kids, what an experience, they were well behaved I guess.
Status Update – April 30, 2016
Up early for a Saturday, Lesa fixed us a good breakfast. Kids and I went to foster training. Amber visit while there. Ate lunch together. Took them to DQ for ice cream after. Naps; Dillan peed on couch again. Tacos for supper, one of the kids favorite meals. More TV than we like.
Status Update – April 29, 2016
Friday! Normal day. I’m tired, kids tired too. Stopped by Dollar General and bought them some M&Ms for a treat – just because. Everyone stuck inside due to rain. Pizza! Tomorrow’s Baseball Jamboree canceled because of rain – looks like a full day of foster training. Easy bedtime!
